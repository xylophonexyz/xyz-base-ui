Be Inspirational.

Merla is designed to make building a website for your brand or business fun and easy. It offers far more flexibility than most existing web design tools, yet is delightfully simple to use. We think you'll fall in love with it's component based graphical interface, intuitive editing features, and minimalist design elements.

Oh, and your users might just fall in love with your new look.

Be Bold.

Merla provides you with everything you need to make your presence known on the web. From custom domains, media management utilities, and hassle-free SEO, you can focus on what matters and know that the rest is taken care of.

Some of the features include:

  • Unlimited pages
  • Support for direct media uploads
  • Support for embeddable content
  • Full HTML customization
  • Custom domains
  • Google SEO, Facebook OpenGraph, Twitter Cards support
  • An OAuth 2.0 based REST API for full featured apps built on top of the platform
  • 24/7 support
  • Pay for what you use
